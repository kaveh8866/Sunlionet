# SunLionet Launch

SunLionet is a two-part system: **Inside** (for end users) and **Outside** (for trusted relays). The goal is simple: keep people connected in high-risk environments without turning the website into a tracking surface.

## What this release focuses on

* Clear separation between the data plane (traffic) and the control plane (coordination)
* Verification-first installs and updates
* Safety language designed for real-world constraints (low bandwidth, intermittent access, VPN usage)
